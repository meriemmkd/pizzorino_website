Welcome to Pizzorino

At Pizzorino, we serve the finest pizzas made with love and the freshest ingredients. Come and experience the taste of authentic Italian cuisine right here in your neighborhood.

About Us

Pizzorino was founded in 1990 by Mario Rossi, a passionate pizza maker from Naples. Our mission is to bring the joy of Italian pizza to your table. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent service and delicious food.
